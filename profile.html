About The Project

About The Project

This website was created as a campaign management platform inspired by the tabletop role-playing game Dungeons & Dragons (D&D). The purpose of the project is to provide a centralized location where both players and Dungeon Masters can manage characters, worlds, and progression without the need for spreadsheets, notebooks, or multiple applications.

Traditional D&D campaigns often require players and Dungeon Masters to keep track of large amounts of information. This includes character statistics, levels, experience points, active effects, buffs, campaign worlds, and story progress. Managing all of this manually can become difficult, especially during long-running campaigns.

This project aims to solve that problem by creating a simple and organized system where all important information can be stored and managed digitally. Players can create characters and join worlds, while Dungeon Masters can manage those worlds and control character progression through a dedicated management panel.

What is Dungeons & Dragons?

Dungeons & Dragons, commonly known as D&D, is a tabletop role-playing game where players create fictional characters and participate in adventures guided by a Dungeon Master (DM). Unlike traditional video games, D&D relies heavily on imagination, storytelling, and decision making.

The Dungeon Master acts as the narrator of the world. They create locations, control non-player characters, describe events, and determine the outcomes of player actions. Players interact with this world using characters they have created themselves.

Characters gain experience throughout their adventures, become stronger over time, unlock new abilities, and develop their own stories based on the choices they make during the campaign.

What Is The Purpose Of This Website?

The primary goal of this website is to simplify campaign management for both players and Dungeon Masters. Instead of storing information in multiple places, everything can be managed through a single platform.

Players can create characters, assign them to worlds, view character information, track experience points, monitor active buffs and effects, and keep track of progression.

Dungeon Masters can create worlds, manage campaigns, view all characters connected to their worlds, award experience points, apply buffs, apply effects, and oversee the development of every character participating in their campaign.

The website is designed to create a clear relationship between players, characters, and Dungeon Masters while maintaining a simple user interface that is easy to learn and use.

How Do I Use The Website?

Create An Account

Begin by creating an account and logging into the system. Once logged in, you will gain access to the dashboard where all major features of the website can be accessed.

Create A Character

Navigate to the character creation page and enter the required information such as character name, race, class, age, and world. Once saved, the character becomes available inside your character collection.

Create A World

Users who wish to act as Dungeon Masters can create worlds through the Worlds page. Each world contains its own collection of linked characters and campaign information.

Link Characters To Worlds

When creating a character, selecting a world automatically links that character to the chosen campaign. Linked characters become visible inside the world page managed by the Dungeon Master.

Manage Characters As A Dungeon Master

Dungeon Masters can select linked characters and access a management panel. Through this panel they can award experience points, increase character levels, apply buffs, remove buffs, apply effects, and remove effects.

Character Progression

Characters gain experience points through the Dungeon Master. Every time a character reaches one hundred experience points, they automatically gain a level and begin progressing toward the next level.

Future Development

Future versions of this project aim to expand the platform beyond campaign management and transform it into a more complete Dungeons & Dragons companion system. Planned features include race-based statistics, character attributes such as Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma, as well as advanced character progression systems that provide meaningful growth as characters level up. Additional gameplay features may include weapons, skills, inventory management, dice rolling systems, combat tracking, and turn-based encounters. These additions will allow players and Dungeon Masters to manage a larger portion of their campaigns directly through the platform. From a technical perspective, future updates are also planned to introduce a dedicated database and backend system. This will replace local browser storage and allow user accounts, characters, and worlds to be stored securely and accessed across multiple devices. The project will also receive improvements to the user interface, including a dedicated home page, improved navigation, headers and footers, and a more polished overall design. The long-term goal is to create a complete digital campaign management platform that combines character management, world building, progression systems, and gameplay tools into a single organized application for both players and Dungeon Masters.

Current limitations

• Data is stored locally in the browser.

• Character sharing is not yet available.

• Multiplayer campaign management is under development.

• Cloud sync is planned for a future update.
